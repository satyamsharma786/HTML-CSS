The Ultimate 5-Minute Guacamole

This recipe delivers a creamy, tangy, and slightly spicy guacamole that pairs perfectly with salty tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite tacos. It is fresh, simple, and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe Hass avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, use a fork or potato masher to gently crush the avocados until they reach your desired consistency.
  2. Fold in the finely diced red onion, chopped cilantro, and lime juice.
  3. Sprinkle the sea salt over the mixture and stir gently to combine all the flavors.
  4. Taste and adjust salt or lime as needed, then serve immediately with your favorite chips.
